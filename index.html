QUIC Deployment in Vietnam

Insights from our 2025 nationwide QUIC scanning study

View Interactive Map

Introduction

This project presents the first large-scale analysis of QUIC protocol deployment across Vietnam. Using tools like ZMap, ZDNS, and QScanner, we scanned over 16 million IPv4 addresses to assess QUIC adoption and visualize its distribution geographically.

Methodology

1. ZMap

Used for high-speed initial scanning of the entire Vietnamese IPv4 address space to detect responsiveness on UDP port 443.

2. ZDNS

Performed reverse DNS lookups to resolve hostnames from responsive IP addresses, aiding in infrastructure identification.

3. QScanner

Executed full QUIC and TLS 1.3 handshakes to verify protocol support, version negotiation, and cryptographic parameters.

Result Overview

8,337 QUIC-Enabled IPs
99.3% IETF QUIC (RFC 9000)
Top ASes VNPT, Viettel, FPT
80.5% AES 128 GCM SHA256
69.4% Server-supported
